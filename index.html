About

11 years in banking. Now I build the tools I wish we'd had.

I'm a Principal Solution Engineer at Salesforce focused on financial services. The job is mostly showing large banks what Tableau and Salesforce can actually do — live, in front of the people who make decisions.

Before Salesforce, I spent 11 years at PNC Bank working on data and analytics across the bank. I know what the problem looks like from the inside — the data was always there, the platform to act on it wasn't.

The problem I keep coming back to is simpler than it sounds: the data usually exists. The question is whether anything surfaces it at the right moment.
